History of Nellai
Karupatti coffee

India is a country known for its Heritage and culture. In the initial days of our lives, our grandma brewed us coffee with freshly made palm jaggery. The ‘Original’ karupatti coffee. Every sip had a unique flavour and was full of richness. Great memories, Great taste. In today’s world, full of trends and growing popularity of western food culture, we want to remain classic and decided to revive the sweet memories. This led to the idea ofNELLAI KARUPATTI COFFEE.





Our Diverse menu

After much research we were successful in bringing tradition and modernisation on the same page.This success motivated us to bring more out of palm Jaggery which paved the way for sweets made of Palm and Sugarcane jaggery. Initially we started with a few sweets and savouries. The Sweets were also a hit among the people. And now we produce 80+ sweets and savouries made out of only Palm and Sugarcane Jaggery.
