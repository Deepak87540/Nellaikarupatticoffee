Steps to open a karupatti coffee franchise



Advance payment is required to book a franchise with us.

After visiting the booked location, we will give you our consent to start the franchise.

After the franchise amount is paid in full, franchise agreement is signed by both parties.

All required equipment will be sent within 3 weeks to the location.

Training will be given to the workers and also the franchise owner.Inventory management procurement details will also be provided.
